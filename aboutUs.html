Coffee & Bean

Coffee & Bean - Flavours of the world in your cup.

Whether it's to start the day, relax in the middle of the afternoon or share a good time at an event, each coffee prepared at Coffee & Bean is an invitation to a unique experience.

Founded by coffee lovers, Coffee & Bean was born with a clear purpose: to transform the simple act of drinking coffee into a unique and unforgettable sensory experience. Inspired by the countries at the heart of high-quality bean production - Brazil, Vietnam, Colombia, Indonesia and Ethiopia - our mission is to bring the world's best flavours straight to your cup.

What we provide

  • Coffee beans: Fresh, ready to be ground on the spot and release all their aroma and flavour.
  • Ground coffees: For those looking for practicality without sacrificing quality.
  • Freshly roasted: Our beans are roasted at the moment of serving, guaranteeing a cup with freshness, intensity and an incomparable flavour.
Turning every break into something special

We want your coffee break to be more than just a moment. We work to create memorable experiences, whether at home, in the office or in catering services. Our commitment is to bring the pleasure and sophistication of coffee wherever you are.

About Us

At Coffee & Bean, we are more than a coffee shop - we are a space where sophistication and delicacy meet to celebrate the essence of coffee. Founded in 2010, our shop was created to provide a unique experience, combining tradition, innovation and love for this drink that connects cultures and people around the world.

By choosing Coffee & Bean, you are part of a community that values origin, respects sustainability and appreciates the unique flavour of each bean. Our coffees are carefully selected from regions such as Brazil, Vietnam, Colombia, Indonesia and Ethiopia, ensuring that each cup is a true celebration of the world's best flavours.

Coffee & Bean: Since 2010, bringing sophistication, delicacy and the world's flavours to your cup with the perfect roast, made especially for you.

Coffee Plantation

Coffee Beans' coffee farms are known for their quality and sustainable bean production. Located in strategic growing regions, these farms combine traditional and modern techniques to guarantee a coffee with a unique, full-bodied flavour.

Roast Machine

The roasting process defines the coffee's profile, such as light, medium or dark roast, influencing acidity, body and sweetness.

Our Team

With a passion for coffee, they master the preparation of drinks, experiment with new flavours and share knowledge with customers, making each experience unique.
