Coffee & Bean

Coffee & Bean - Flavours of the world in your cup.

Whether it's to start the day, relax in the middle of the afternoon or share a good time at an event, each coffee prepared at Coffee & Bean is an invitation to a unique experience.

Founded by coffee lovers, Coffee & Bean was born with a clear purpose: to transform the simple act of drinking coffee into a unique and unforgettable sensory experience. Inspired by the countries at the heart of high-quality bean production - Brazil, Vietnam, Colombia, Indonesia and Ethiopia - our mission is to bring the world's best flavours straight to your cup. From the coffee plantations in the mountains of Minas Gerais in Brazil to the volcanic plateaus of Indonesia, each bean that arrives at Coffee Shopp carries stories of fertile lands, centuries-old traditions and the dedicated work of farmers who make coffee a symbol of passion and excellence.

What we provide

We at Coffee & Bean think that every little thing counts. Our selection includes:

  • Coffee beans: Fresh, ready to be ground on the spot and release all their aroma and flavour.
  • Ground coffees: For those looking for practicality without sacrificing quality.
  • Freshly roasted: Our beans are roasted at the moment of serving, guaranteeing a cup with freshness, intensity and an incomparable flavour.
Turning every break into something special

We want your coffee break to be more than just a moment. We work to create memorable experiences, whether at home, in the office or in catering services. Our commitment is to bring the pleasure and sophistication of coffee wherever you are.

About Us

At Coffee & Bean, we are more than a Coffee & Bean - we are a space where sophistication and delicacy meet to celebrate the essence of coffee. Founded in 2010, our shop was created to provide a unique experience, combining tradition, innovation and love for this drink that connects cultures and people around the world.

Every detail at Coffee & Bean reflects our passion and commitment to excellence. From the cosy and elegant atmosphere to the unmistakable aroma of freshly roasted beans, we want every visit to our shop to be an unforgettable sensory experience. Our state-of-the-art roasting machine, the centrepiece of the shop, is proof of our care. It transforms each grain into a masterpiece, guaranteeing freshness, flavour and a presentation that delights the eye and the palate.

By choosing Coffee & Bean, you are part of a community that values origin, respects sustainability and appreciates the unique flavour of each bean. Our coffees are carefully selected from regions such as Brazil, Vietnam, Colombia, Indonesia and Ethiopia, ensuring that each cup is a true celebration of the world's best flavours.

Come and discover how we turn coffee into art and every moment into a special memory.
